Unitrans 2020-2021 Service Change Proposals

Unitrans proposes service changes for the 2020-2021 academic year starting August 3, 2020 that will improve your bus ride. Improvement proposals focus on making your ride faster and more reliable. To provide comments, you may email us at comments@unitrans.ucdavis.edu, call 530-752-2877, or mail Unitrans, 1 Shields Avenue, 5 South Hall, Davis, CA 95616. The comment period will close on Friday, May 22, 2020.

Reliability Improvements

All Door Boarding In order to reduce boarding times and reduce travel times, Unitrans proposes allowing customers with passes including undergraduate registration cards to board the bus through the front OR rear doors. Only cash payers and 10-ride ticket holders will be required to board through the front doors. We trust our valued customers to continue to pay for their trips. As a reminder, Unitrans already entrusts customers at the Silo and Memorial Union Bus Terminals to pay their fare. Unitrans is not free at terminals. Pilot will last for up to a year and will evaluate service improvements and whether or not fare revenue is lost. (Add graphic from Sam)

Morning Schedule Update on All Lines

Unitrans proposes shifting all morning schedules five minutes earlier between 6:30 AM and 9:00 AM. The proposal will provide extra time for trips between 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM when buses are the most crowded and delayed by morning traffic. Departures starting at 9:00 AM will remain teh same as today. Drop Off Only Stops on the G, J, and V Lines

To improve travel times, Unitrans proposes drop off only stops at outbound (away from campus) bus stops on Alvarado Avenue and Sycamore Lane for the G/J lines and on Hutchison Drive at Sage Street and Sage Street at West Village Square on the V line. Customers will not be allowed to board at drop off only stops. Customers will need to board at the inbound bus stop across the street. Capacity Improvements

More Morning A & V Line Service Unitrans proposes adding a capacity bus on the A line for all trips between 7:30 AM and 10:00 AM serving Fifth Street at Cantrill, Pole Line Road across from the Post Office, and the Department of Motor Vehicles to reduce crowding in light of the Sterling Fifth Apartments opening.
Unitrans proposes adding additional V line trips (up to three more trips per hour) from 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM to reduce crowding. The additional trips would only serve inbound bus stops on Tilia at Celadon Street and Sage Street. (insert A and V line circle graphic)

New Evening Trips

Unitrans proposes new 7:10 PM Z line and new 8:10 PM K and Z line trips to reduce crowding on the A and K lines.
Afternoon Schedule Change on the V Line Unitrans proposes adjusting the 25 minute after the hour afternoon and evening Silo Terminal V Line departures to 20 minutes after the hour to better meet customer demand. Click here for sample schedule. (insert clock graphic)

Reallocation of Buses

Unitrans proposes eliminating low ridership 6:20 PM D, G, J, and W lines and 6:25 PM V line trips. These buses will be reallocated and used on overcrowded and late 6:35 PM trips.

Reminder from 2019

A/O/Z Line Westbound Reroute Near the Davis Amtrak Station As approved in April 2019, Unitrans will implement the approved A/O/Z line westbound reroute near the Davis Amtrak Station if safety incidents increase.
